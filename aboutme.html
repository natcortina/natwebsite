Dear human, happy you made it here !

Born and raised in Mexico City, I am a designer focused on creative technology based in Manhattan NY. I am very curious and growth-oriented, a people person for sure, really good communicator and someone who likes a dynamic and fast paced work environment (I am an Aries).

My main focus in my projects/designs/animations are mindfullness, self-love, and everyday experiences.

I would like further in my projects, to keep mixing the serious with comedy. so taboo or complex topics can be approached and understood by people more easily.

I love to take a surrealist approach, as well as a minimalistic and "homemade" aesthetic.

I want people to feel at ease, understood and warped around what I do, for people to be safe and listened, and for my designs to speak for things that people struggle to talk about.
